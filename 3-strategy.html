With a goal in place, we can now focus on how we are going to achieve it.

Strategy overview

At a high level, the strategy will cover four main areas: (1) publishing content for the firm's target audience, (2) offering website subscriptions to build up the marketing automation database of contacts whose digital activity can be tracked, (3) distributing content to members of the marketing automation database, and (4) providing the sales team with information about the digital activity of their clients and prospects who are members of the marketing automation database.

Marketing automation database

The strategy is centred around the firm's database of contacts stored within the marketing automation system. We want to build this database as much as we can – which will include both new prospects and existing clients – because we will be able to track a contacts engagement with the firm’s digital content (website, emails, documents, and so on) at an individual level.

Strategy implementation

The strategy is implemented through a series of steps:

  1. Firm research. Perform research on and document important aspects of the firm.
  2. Groundwork. Complete important preparatory work and start regularly publishing blog content on the firm's website (if you aren't already).
  3. Lead generation and management. Set up the digital marketing infrastructure to grow and manage the marketing automation database and notify salespeople of interesting digital activity.
  4. Content distribution. Start distributing content to segments of the marketing database via email, to encourage digital activity.
  5. Expansion projects. Undertake any relevant projects, ensuring they are integrated with the work done so far.

Firm research

A key initial step – and something I have not seen attempted – involves comprehensively researching and documenting key aspects of your firm (such as its investment strategies, products, target audience, and so on). This research allows digital marketing teams to properly understand the firm they work for and tailor their approach.
